Aggregated from **267 accepted oral papers**. Generated by `aggregate.py` from `features/*.json`. Source papers were scraped from arXiv by matching the ACL 2026 oral list (267/478 oral papers had arXiv preprints with LaTeX sources).

Use these as an ACL yardstick. Each numeric field is the corpus median and inter-quartile range; if a flagged field is *domain-sensitive*, the range varies by sub-task (LLMs vs generation vs retrieval vs parsing), so deviations are defensible with a domain-specific reason, not a violation of the convention. Quotes are verbatim excerpts (attributed by arxiv id) — do not paraphrase.
